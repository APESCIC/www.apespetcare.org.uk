The Pet Care Clinic    

Without your generous donations, the APES CIC Pet Care Clinic could not deliver vital veterinary services to pet owners facing financial hardship. Your gift helps us cover the cost of treatments, medications, and community outreach, keeping pets healthy and families together.


Why Your Support Matters
• Ensures affordable care for low-income pet owners
• Funds consultations, surgeries, vaccinations, and medications
• Enables education programs on responsible pet ownership

How to Donate

  1. Choose a one-time or recurring gift that fits your budget
  2. To donate equipment or supplies, email info@apes.org.uk

Every contribution—large or small—makes a real difference to pets in need. Thank you for standing with APES CIC and supporting our mission.

Helping people in poverty or low incomes to keep their pets healthy and safe.
