Lab Services

At APES Pet Care Clinic, we are committed to providing top-notch care for your exotic pets. In partnership with a renowned laboratory in Kent, we offer cost-effective testing services that help you avoid the higher costs typically associated with veterinary clinics. Our collaboration ensures that you receive accurate and timely test results, all while maintaining the highest standards of quality care.

We understand the importance of seamless communication between pet owners and veterinary professionals. Therefore, upon request, we can conveniently transfer your pet's test results directly to your veterinarian, ensuring that your pet's medical records are comprehensive and up-to-date.

Visit us and experience the dedicated service that prioritizes your pet's well-being, ensuring they remain healthy and happy. Whether you're a seasoned pet owner or new to the world of exotic pets, our clinic is here to support your needs with expertise and compassion. Welcome to a community that truly cares for your exotic family members!
