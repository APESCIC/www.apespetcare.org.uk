- Reptile Care Services
- Please note we are not a VET we are a pet clinic that can deal with routine care, treatments and some first aid.
- We try to keep our fees as low as possible, calculating time, and the situation.
- Reptile Services
- You can find a number of services provided below for reptiles.
- Shedding Aid
- Get your dog treated for £10.00 Per Pet, we will help with removal of stubbon shedding and eye caps if needed.
- Parasite Removal
- Get your reptile treated for mites by us. £30.00 Per Pet. Treatment can take up to 7 days but sometimes more so we charge £49 per 7 days while in for treatment.
- Fang Removal
- We remove fangs when become disloged or when they cause issues with the mouth. We charge £10.00 Per Fang. Snakes Only!
- Respritory Infections
- We can treat your reptile for common respritory infections. Treatment is £15.00 Per Pet. We also charge £49.00 per 7 days while in for treatment.
- Nail Clipping
- Get your dog treated for £5.00 per pet. Please note agressive dogs can not be done. We will refer you to a vet that can sedate.
